The marriage was dissolved in 2021. Notice of Filing of Decree of Dissolution (Maricopa County Superior Court case FN2020-XXXXXX, decree dated 2021-03-15) was recorded March 30, 2021 [20210200001]. Pursuant to the decree, Carlos executed a quit-claim deed to Sofia recorded April 22, 2021 [20210200002]. A quit-claim deed conveys only whatever interest the grantor has, with no warranty. Sofia now holds record title as her sole and separate property.

On August 15, 2022, the servicing on the 2016 deed of trust was transferred from Chase to Mr. Cooper by assignment [20220200001]. The assignment modifies the beneficiary of record but does not alter the borrower list on the note; Carlos remains jointly liable.
