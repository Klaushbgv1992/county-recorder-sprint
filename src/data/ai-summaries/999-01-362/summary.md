# Chain of title: 3650 E Palmer St, Gilbert AZ (probate scenario)

The subject parcel is Lot 44, Seville Parcel 3, a 95-lot Shea Homes subdivision. Every instrument on this parcel in the corpus is demo-only synthesized — disclosed per-document in the Proof Drawer — so the chain below illustrates examiner workflow rather than reproducing a real recorded history.

By warranty deed recorded April 15, 2008, Shea Homes Arizona LP conveyed the property to Robert K and Lilian M Chen, husband and wife, as community property with right of survivorship [20080200001]. Concurrently, the Chens executed a purchase-money deed of trust in favor of Wells Fargo Bank, with MERS as beneficiary as nominee [20080200002]. That loan was paid off in 2019 and fully reconveyed on March 22, 2019 [20190200001].

Lilian M Chen died April 10, 2023. Because the 2008 vesting was community property with right of survivorship, title passed to Robert by operation of law; no probate was required for the first death. The Affidavit of Death of Joint Tenant recorded June 20, 2023 [20230200001] cures the record and attaches the death certificate as evidence.

Robert K Chen died January 8, 2024, holding sole title. His estate was probated in Maricopa County Superior Court Probate Division, case PB2024-XXXXXX. By Personal Representative's Deed recorded August 5, 2024 [20240200001], the estate — acting through David James Chen as Personal Representative — conveyed fee title to David James Chen, son and sole devisee. A Personal Representative's Deed carries only the warranties of a Special Warranty Deed and only as against the acts of the decedent or the Personal Representative.
