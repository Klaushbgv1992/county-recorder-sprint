# Chain of Title Summary — Tract A, Seville Parcel 3

## What this parcel actually is

This record is for **Tract A of Seville Parcel 3**, not an individual home lot. Tract A is one of the common-area tracts (A through H) inside the subdivision, and it is currently held by the **Seville Homeowners Association**.

## How the subdivision came into being

In February 2001, Shea Homes Limited Partnership recorded the **final plat for Seville Parcel 3**, creating Lots 1–95 and Tracts A–H on roughly 23.6 acres in Gilbert [20010093192]. That plat is the foundational document — it dedicates the public utility easements, building setbacks, and HOA-maintained tracts that run with the land.

Later that year, on September 17, 2001, the subdivision's registered land surveyor recorded an **Affidavit of Correction** back to Shea Homes to fix an error in the original plat (a signature-block issue) [20010849180]. That correction is cross-referenced in the legal description of this parcel, so both documents travel together.
