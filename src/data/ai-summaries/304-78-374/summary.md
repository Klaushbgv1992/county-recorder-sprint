The subject parcel is Lot 52, Seville Parcel 3, a 95-lot Shea Homes subdivision platted in the South half of Section 23, Township 2 South, Range 6 East, recorded in Book 554 of Maps, Page 19 [20010093192]. The plat dedicates public utility easements, HOA-maintained tracts (A–H), and building setbacks that run with every lot in the subdivision. An Affidavit of Correction recorded September 2001 cured a signature-block defect in the original plat and is cross-referenced in every downstream legal description for the parcel.

Contemporaneously with acquisition, Warner executed a deed of trust to Academy Mortgage Corporation [20130087109]. On February 14, 2019, Warner executed a junior HELOC deed of trust in favor of Desert Schools Federal Credit Union [20190100001].
